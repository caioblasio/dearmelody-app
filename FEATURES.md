# Project Brief: Song Diary

## 1. Project Overview
**Song Diary** is a unique journaling application that transforms personal reflections into personalized musical compositions. By bridging the gap between written word and auditory expression, the app provides users with a multisensory way to capture and revisit their moods and memories.

## 2. Core Value Proposition
- **Emotional Translation:** Uses AI to interpret the emotional nuance of a user's day and translate it into a unique song.
- **Mindful Reflection:** Encourages a daily habit of journaling through a novel, rewarding creative output.
- **Musical Archive:** Creates a chronological "soundtrack of your life" that users can browse and revisit.

## 3. Target Audience
- **Journalers & Reflective Individuals:** People looking for deeper ways to process their daily experiences.
- **Music Lovers:** Those who find emotional resonance in music and want a more personalized connection to it.
- **Tech Enthusiasts:** Early adopters interested in creative applications of generative AI.

## 4. Key Features
### Phase 1: Core Experience
- **Journaling Interface (New Entry):** A minimal, distraction-free text editor for daily reflections.
- **AI Song Generation:** Seamless integration with an external endpoint to generate lyrics and audio based on the text input.
- **Immersive Player:** A dedicated visualization screen for the generated song, including synchronized lyrics.
- **Chronological Archive (Past Melodies):** A searchable history of all past musical entries.
- **Cross-Device Support:** Fully responsive design for Desktop and Mobile experiences.

## 5. User Flows
1. **Onboarding:** User arrives at the Login screen and enters their credentials.
2. **Journaling:** User lands on the 'New Entry' screen, writes about their day, and clicks "Generate My Song".
3. **Consumption:** User is taken to the 'Your Melody' screen where the song plays and lyrics are displayed.
4. **Retention:** User visits the 'Archive' to browse through previous days and listen to past melodies.

## 6. Design Principles (Melodic Reflection)
- **Serenity & Calm:** Using a soft, light-filled palette (Noto Serif typography, subtle stone and indigo tones) to create a safe space for reflection.
- **Journal Aesthetic:** Incorporating elements that evoke physical stationery—generous whitespace, serif fonts, and elegant borders.
- **Minimalism:** Removing unnecessary noise (like gradients) to keep the focus on the user's words and the resulting music.

## 7. Success Metrics
- **Daily Active Users (DAU):** Percentage of users returning daily to log an entry.
- **Creation Rate:** Number of songs generated per user per week.
- **Session Duration:** Time spent in the archive revisiting past melodies.
